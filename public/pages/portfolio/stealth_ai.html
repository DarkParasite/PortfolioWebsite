Stealth AI

Blueprint AI Unreal Engine

June 2023 - July 2023

I worked on this project for a client in Fiverr. It has so many AI mechanics implemented with AI Perception, Behaviour Trees, Blackboard, EQS, Tasks and other AI related features from Unreal Engine. Since many number of AI will be at the scene in a given time, many data calculated by AI is shared between them through a common interface manager, optimizing the performance. There are so many features I have listed them below.

Things Implemented

Patrolling (Single, Ping-Pong and Loop)

Custom Patrol Points and Wait Times

Detecting Player with Detection Meter

AI search player if half detected

Taking AI hostage

Kill hostage or call out other enemies

AI Noise Detection

AI tries to look and flush player on hearing noise

Melee Combat with Combat Mechanics

AI generating and sharing Cover Points

Move between Cover Points and Fire at Player

Coordination between AI to Switch Covers

AI Throw Grenade

AI Sneaking up on Player for Melee Combat

Knock down AI

AI reviving AI

AI calling out death of other AI

AI commit suicide

AI managing common interface

AI groups for different area in game
