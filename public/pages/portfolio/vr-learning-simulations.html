VR Learning Simulations

Blueprint VR Unreal Engine

January 2024

Description and Showcase

This is a VR Learning Simulation Platform which has two playable levels.

First is the Solar System Simulation, which very accurate simulates things like Rotation, Revolution, Planet Size (Diameters), relative distance of each heavenly body from each other etc. Player can move between different platform, view information about the planet in their Hand Held 3D HUD, change simulation speed and experience the 3D planet simulation in VR. Custom shaders have been written to accurately depict all directional lighting, Sun's surface Earth's day and night cycle, Jupiter's moving gas atmosphere, Saturn's ring etc.

Second project is the Human Anatomy Simulation, an interactive experience where users can explore and engage with various human organs in detail. Players can pick up organs, manipulate them by moving and scaling, and examine different areas closely. Each part of the organ is labeled with 3D text, providing educational insights and helping users identify and learn about each specific region.

Key Features and Mechanics

Planet Rotation and Revolution

Adjustable Simulation Speed

Sun surface shader

Earth day/night cycle shader

Jupiter's moving atmosphere shader

Interactable human organs

3D labeled organ region/part
